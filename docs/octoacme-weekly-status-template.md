# OctoAcme — Weekly Project Status Update Template

## Purpose
Provide a consistent, lightweight format for communicating project health to stakeholders and team members each week.

**Owner:** Project Manager (PM)
**Audience:** Stakeholders, PdM, Engineering Manager/Tech Lead, and delivery team
**Cadence:** Weekly (recommended: every Monday or Friday)

---

## Status Update Template

```
Project: [Project Name]
Week of: [YYYY-MM-DD]
Status: 🟢 On Track | 🟡 At Risk | 🔴 Off Track
Prepared by: [PM Name]
```

### Summary
> One or two sentences summarizing overall project health and any notable developments this week.

### Progress This Week
- [ ] [Milestone or task completed]
- [ ] [Feature or deliverable shipped]
- [ ] [Key decision made — link to Decision Log if applicable]

### Planned for Next Week
- [ ] [Upcoming milestone or task]
- [ ] [Deliverable expected]
- [ ] [Key meeting or review scheduled]

### Risks & Blockers
| Risk/Blocker | Impact | Owner | Mitigation | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Description] | High/Med/Low | [Name] | [Action] | Open/Mitigated/Closed |

> For full risk details, refer to the [Risk Register](octoacme-risk-register-template.md).

### Metrics Snapshot
| Metric | Target | Actual | Trend |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Velocity / Story Points] | [target] | [actual] | ↑ / → / ↓ |
| [Defect escape rate] | [target] | [actual] | ↑ / → / ↓ |
| [Key product metric] | [target] | [actual] | ↑ / → / ↓ |

### Decisions Needed / Asks
- [ ] [Decision or approval needed — from whom, by when]
- [ ] [Dependency or unblocking action required]

> Log approved decisions in the [Decision Log](octoacme-decision-log-template.md).

---

## Usage Notes
- Use the 🟢/🟡/🔴 status indicator to give stakeholders an immediate read on project health.
- Keep the update concise — ideally readable in under 2 minutes.
- Link to the Risk Register and Decision Log for supporting detail rather than duplicating content.
- Archive completed weekly updates in the project repo or wiki.
